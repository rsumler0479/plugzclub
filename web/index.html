What is a membership club ?

Membership clubs are organizations that convert customers of a dispensary/smoke shop to members of a club. This type of operational structure helps keep the city from cracking down on your shop without legal due process.

Incorporating membership clubs are also a good way to:

  • Collect customer data for greater marketing insights
  • Establish brand identity with the community you service
